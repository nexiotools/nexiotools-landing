Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your content is processed securely and never stored. We only retain your email address for access management.

Who we are

Nexio Tools is operated by an individual based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at nexio.tools@gmail.com.

What data we collect

We collect your email address when you purchase access to one of our tools. This is stored securely and used solely to verify your access status.

What we do NOT collect

We do not store, log or retain any content you submit to our tools -- meeting notes, CVs, contracts or salary data. Your content is sent to the Anthropic API for processing and immediately discarded after the response is returned. No content is ever written to our database.

Anthropic API

Content submitted to BrieflyAI, CraftCV and Contract2Check is processed via the Anthropic API. Anthropic does not use API data to train their models by default. See anthropic.com/privacy for full details.

Cookies

We use cookies solely to remember your access status locally in your browser. We use Google AdSense on Looncheck which may place cookies for advertising purposes. You can manage your cookie preferences via the consent banner shown on first visit.

Your rights under GDPR

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. To request deletion of your email address from our system, contact us at nexio.tools@gmail.com and we will remove it within 48 hours.

Data retention

Email addresses are stored for the duration of your access period and automatically deleted upon expiry. Starter access expires after 3 months, Pro after 1 year. Lifetime access is retained until deletion is requested.

Third parties

We use the following third-party services:

Lemon Squeezy -- payment processing. See lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Upstash -- secure Redis database for access management. Data stored in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). See upstash.com/trust/privacy.pdf.

Anthropic -- AI processing. See anthropic.com/privacy.

Google AdSense -- advertising on Looncheck only. See policies.google.com/privacy.

Vercel -- hosting infrastructure. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests: nexio.tools@gmail.com
